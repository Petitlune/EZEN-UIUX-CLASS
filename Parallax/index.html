Night view of world cities

Seoul

Namsan Seoul Tower, also known as N Seoul Tower, offers a panoramic view of the city. At night, the tower itself is illuminated in various colors, and visitors can enjoy a 360-degree view of the sparkling city lights from the observation deck.

Osaka

This historic tower in the Shinsekai district is beautifully lit at night. The surrounding area is filled with retro-style shops and restaurants, giving it a nostalgic feel.

London

As the tallest building in the UK, The Shard provides an unparalleled view of London's nightscape. The observation deck offers a panoramic view of the city illuminated below.

Bangkok

Sky Bar, one of the highest rooftop bars in the world, offers an extraordinary night view of Bangkok. The city lights, combined with the view of the Chao Phraya River, make for an unforgettable experience.
